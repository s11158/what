Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026 · Applies to the WhatsApp bot “WhatApp?” (+44 7529 450098) and the website what.b3.gg.

What the bot processes

When you forward a voice message, video or text to the bot, the content is processed automatically for one purpose only: to transcribe the speech and translate it into the language you selected. Processing is performed by our server and by third-party processors: speech recognition by Groq (Whisper) and machine translation services.

What we store

We store your phone number together with: your selected translation language, a flag that you have received the welcome message, and a counter of processed messages. For service statistics we keep aggregate daily counters and a short technical log of recent events (message type, detected language, a short text preview). We do not store your audio or video files — media is deleted from our server immediately after transcription.

What we don't do

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers. We do not use your messages to train models. We do not send you marketing messages; the bot only replies to what you send it.

Website analytics

what.b3.gg counts page views and button clicks in aggregate, without cookies and without collecting personal data or IP addresses.

Data deletion

To delete your data (language choice and counters), send the word delete to the bot or contact us and we will remove all records linked to your number.
