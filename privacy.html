Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026 · Applies to the WhatsApp bot “WhatApp?” (+44 7529 450098) and the website what.b3.gg.

What the bot processes

When you forward a voice message, video or text to the bot, the content is processed automatically for one purpose only: to transcribe the speech and translate it into the language you selected. Processing is performed by our server and by third-party processors: speech recognition by Groq (Whisper) and machine translation services.

What we store

We store your phone number together with: your selected translation language, a flag that you have received the welcome message, and a counter of processed messages.

We also keep a message log used to operate, debug and improve the service. It contains the text of what you send the bot, the transcripts of your voice and video messages, and the translations the bot replies with, together with the time and your phone number. This log is kept indefinitely until you ask us to delete it (see below). It is accessible only to the operator of the service.

We do not store your audio or video files themselves — media is deleted from our server immediately after transcription.

What we don't do

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers. We do not use your messages to train models. We do not send you marketing messages; the bot only replies to what you send it.

Please keep in mind

Because transcripts and translations are stored, avoid forwarding the bot messages containing passwords, card numbers, medical records or other sensitive information you would not want kept. Send the word delete at any time to erase everything we hold about you.

Website analytics

what.b3.gg counts page views and button clicks in aggregate, without cookies and without collecting personal data or IP addresses.

Data deletion

Send the word delete to the bot and we remove everything linked to your number: your profile (language choice, counters) and your entire message log, including transcripts and translations. The deletion runs immediately and cannot be undone.
